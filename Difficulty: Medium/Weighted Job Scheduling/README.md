<ul style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px 0px 24px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-size: 18px; vertical-align: baseline; list-style-position: outside; list-style-image: initial; display: flex; flex-direction: column; color: #273239; font-family: Nunito, sans-serif; letter-spacing: 0.162px; background-color: #ffffff;">
<li style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px 0px 0px 24px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; vertical-align: baseline; text-align: left; line-height: 1.58;" value="1"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; vertical-align: baseline; font-family: 'andale mono', monospace;">jobs[i][0] : Start time of the job</span></li>
<li style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px 0px 0px 24px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; vertical-align: baseline; text-align: left; line-height: 1.58;" value="2"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; vertical-align: baseline; font-family: 'andale mono', monospace;">jobs[i][1] : End time of the job</span></li>
<li style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px 0px 0px 24px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; vertical-align: baseline; text-align: left; line-height: 1.58;" value="3"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; vertical-align: baseline; font-family: 'andale mono', monospace;">jobs[i][2] : Profit earned by completing the job</span></li>
</ul>
<p dir="ltr" style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px 0px 10px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-size: 18px; vertical-align: baseline; color: #273239; font-family: Nunito, sans-serif; letter-spacing: 0.162px; background-color: #ffffff;"><span style="font-family: 'andale mono', monospace;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; vertical-align: baseline;">Find the&nbsp;</span><strong style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; vertical-align: baseline;"><strong style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; vertical-align: baseline;">maximum profit</strong></strong><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; vertical-align: baseline;">&nbsp;you can earn by scheduling non-overlapping jobs.</span></span></p>
<p dir="ltr" style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px 0px 10px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-size: 18px; vertical-align: baseline; color: #273239; font-family: Nunito, sans-serif; letter-spacing: 0.162px; background-color: #ffffff;"><span style="font-family: 'andale mono', monospace;"><strong style="letter-spacing: 0.162px; box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; vertical-align: baseline;"><strong style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; vertical-align: baseline;">Note</strong></strong><span style="letter-spacing: 0.162px; box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; vertical-align: baseline;">: Two jobs are said to be non-overlapping if the end time of one job is less than or equal to the start time of the next job. If a job ends at time X, another job can start exactly at time X.</span></span></p>
<p dir="ltr" style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px 0px 10px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-size: 18px; vertical-align: baseline; color: #273239; font-family: Nunito, sans-serif; letter-spacing: 0.162px; background-color: #ffffff;"><span style="font-family: 'andale mono', monospace;"><strong style="font-size: 14pt; letter-spacing: 0.162px;">Examples:</strong></span></p>
<pre><span style="font-family: 'andale mono', monospace; font-size: 14pt;"><strong>Input: </strong><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; vertical-align: baseline; color: #273239; letter-spacing: 0.162px; text-wrap-mode: wrap; background-color: #f9f9f9;">jobs[][] =  [[1, 2, 50], </span><br style="box-sizing: border-box; color: #273239; font-style: italic; letter-spacing: 0.162px; text-wrap-mode: wrap; background-color: #f9f9f9;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; vertical-align: baseline; color: #273239; letter-spacing: 0.162px; text-wrap-mode: wrap; background-color: #f9f9f9;">                 [3, 5, 20],</span><br style="box-sizing: border-box; color: #273239; font-style: italic; letter-spacing: 0.162px; text-wrap-mode: wrap; background-color: #f9f9f9;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; vertical-align: baseline; color: #273239; letter-spacing: 0.162px; text-wrap-mode: wrap; background-color: #f9f9f9;">                 [6, 19, 100],</span><br style="box-sizing: border-box; color: #273239; font-style: italic; letter-spacing: 0.162px; text-wrap-mode: wrap; background-color: #f9f9f9;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; vertical-align: baseline; color: #273239; letter-spacing: 0.162px; text-wrap-mode: wrap; background-color: #f9f9f9;">                 [2, 100, 200]] </span>
<strong>Output: </strong><span style="color: #273239; letter-spacing: 0.162px; text-wrap-mode: wrap; background-color: #f9f9f9;">250</span>
<strong>Explanation: </strong>The first and fourth jobs with the time range [1, 2] and [2, 100] can be chosen to give maximum profit of 50 + 200 = 250.</span></pre>
<pre><span style="font-family: 'andale mono', monospace; font-size: 14pt;"><strong>Input: </strong><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; vertical-align: baseline; color: #273239; letter-spacing: 0.162px; text-wrap-mode: wrap; background-color: #f9f9f9;">jobs[][] =  [[1, 3, 60], </span><br style="box-sizing: border-box; color: #273239; font-style: italic; letter-spacing: 0.162px; text-wrap-mode: wrap; background-color: #f9f9f9;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; vertical-align: baseline; color: #273239; letter-spacing: 0.162px; text-wrap-mode: wrap; background-color: #f9f9f9;">                 [2, 5, 50],</span><br style="box-sizing: border-box; color: #273239; font-style: italic; letter-spacing: 0.162px; text-wrap-mode: wrap; background-color: #f9f9f9;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; vertical-align: baseline; color: #273239; letter-spacing: 0.162px; text-wrap-mode: wrap; background-color: #f9f9f9;">                 [4, 6, 70],</span><br style="box-sizing: border-box; color: #273239; font-style: italic; letter-spacing: 0.162px; text-wrap-mode: wrap; background-color: #f9f9f9;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; vertical-align: baseline; color: #273239; letter-spacing: 0.162px; text-wrap-mode: wrap; background-color: #f9f9f9;">                 [5, 7, 30]] </span>
<strong>Output: </strong>130
<strong>Explanation: </strong>The first and third jobs with the time range [1, 3] and [4, 6] can be chosen to give maximum profit of 60 + 70 = 130.</span></pre>
<p><span style="font-size: 14pt; font-family: 'andale mono', monospace;"><strong>Constraints:</strong><br>1 ≤ jobs.size() ≤ 10<sup>5</sup><sup><br></sup>1 ≤ jobs[i][0] &lt; jobs[i][1] ≤ 10<sup>9</sup></span><br><span style="font-size: 14pt; font-family: 'andale mono', monospace;">1 ≤ jobs[i][2] ≤ 10<sup>4</sup></span></p></div><br><p><span style=font-size:18px><strong>Topic Tags : </strong><br><code>Dynamic Programming</code>&nbsp;<code>Binary Search</code>&nbsp;<code>Recursion</code>&nbsp;
