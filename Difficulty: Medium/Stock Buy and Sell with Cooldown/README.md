<h2><a href="https://www.geeksforgeeks.org/problems/buy-stock-with-cooldown/1">Stock Buy and Sell with Cooldown</a></h2><h3>Difficulty Level : Difficulty: Medium</h3><hr><div class="problems_problem_content__Xm_eO"><p data-start="488" data-end="784"><span style="font-family: 'andale mono', monospace; font-size: 14pt;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; color: #273932; letter-spacing: 0.21px; background-color: #ffffff;">Given an array&nbsp;</span><span style="box-sizing: border-box; font-weight: bolder; color: #273932; letter-spacing: 0.21px; background-color: #ffffff;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; font-weight: bolder;">arr[]</span></span><span style="box-sizing: border-box; color: #273932; letter-spacing: 0.21px; background-color: #ffffff;">, where the&nbsp;</span><span style="box-sizing: border-box; font-weight: bolder; color: #273932; letter-spacing: 0.21px; background-color: #ffffff;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; font-weight: bolder;">ith</span></span><span style="box-sizing: border-box; color: #273932; letter-spacing: 0.21px; background-color: #ffffff;">&nbsp;element of arr[] represents the price of a stock on the&nbsp;</span><span style="box-sizing: border-box; font-weight: bolder; color: #273932; letter-spacing: 0.21px; background-color: #ffffff;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; font-weight: bolder;">ith day</span></span><span style="box-sizing: border-box; color: #273932; letter-spacing: 0.21px; background-color: #ffffff;">&nbsp;(all prices are non-negative integers). Find the <strong>maximum profit</strong> you can make by buying and selling stocks such that after selling a stock, you cannot buy again on the <strong>next day</strong> (i.e., there is a one-day cooldown).</span></span></p>
<p data-start="488" data-end="784"><span style="font-family: 'andale mono', monospace; font-size: 14pt;"><strong><span style="white-space: pre;">Examples:</span></strong></span></p>
<pre><span style="font-family: 'andale mono', monospace; font-size: 14pt;"><strong>Input: </strong><span style="color: #273932; letter-spacing: 0.21px; text-wrap-mode: wrap; background-color: #f9f9f9;">arr[] = [0, 2, 1, 2, 3]</span>
<strong>Output: </strong>3
<strong>Explanation: </strong><span style="color: #273932; letter-spacing: 0.21px; text-wrap-mode: wrap; background-color: #f9f9f9;">You first buy on day 1, sell on day 2 then cool down, then buy on day 4, and sell on day 5. The total profit earned is (2-0) + (3-2) = 3, which is the maximum achievable profit.</span></span></pre>
<pre><span style="font-family: 'andale mono', monospace; font-size: 14pt;"><strong>Input: </strong><span style="color: #273932; letter-spacing: 0.21px; text-wrap-mode: wrap; background-color: #f9f9f9;"> arr[] = [3, 1, 6, 1, 2, 4]</span>
<strong>Output: </strong>7
<strong>Explanation: </strong><span style="color: #273932; letter-spacing: 0.21px; text-wrap-mode: wrap; background-color: #f9f9f9;">You first buy on day 2 and sell on day 3 then cool down, then again you buy on day 5 and then sell on day 6. Clearly, the total profit earned is (6-1) + (4-2) = 7, which is the maximum achievable profit.</span><br></span></pre>
<p><span style="font-family: 'andale mono', monospace; font-size: 14pt;"><strong>Constraint:</strong><br>1&nbsp;<span style="background-color: #ffffff; color: #1e2229;">≤&nbsp;</span><span style="background-color: #f9f9f9; color: #273932; letter-spacing: 0.21px;">arr</span>.size()&nbsp;<span style="background-color: #ffffff; color: #1e2229;">≤ </span>10<sup>5</sup></span><br><span style="font-family: 'andale mono', monospace; font-size: 14pt;">1&nbsp;<span style="background-color: #ffffff; color: #1e2229;">≤&nbsp;</span><span style="background-color: #f9f9f9; color: #273932; letter-spacing: 0.21px;">arr</span>[i] <span style="color: #1e2229; background-color: #ffffff;">≤ </span>10<sup>4</sup></span></p></div><br><p><span style=font-size:18px><strong>Topic Tags : </strong><br><code>Dynamic Programming</code>&nbsp;
