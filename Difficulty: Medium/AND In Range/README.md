<h2><a href="https://www.geeksforgeeks.org/problems/and-operation5726/1">AND In Range</a></h2><h3>Difficulty Level : Difficulty: Medium</h3><hr><div class="problems_problem_content__Xm_eO"><p><span style="font-size: 18px;">You are given two integers <strong>l</strong> and <strong>r</strong>. Find the result after applying the series of <strong>Bitwise AND</strong> ( &amp; ) operation on every natural number between the range l to r (including both).</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong>Examples:</strong></span></p>
<pre><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong>Input: </strong>l = 8, r = 13
<strong>Output: </strong>8
<strong>Explanation: 
</strong></span><span style="font-size: 18px;">8 AND 9 AND 10 AND 11 AND 12 AND 13 = 8.</span>
</pre>
<pre><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong>Input: </strong>l = 2, r = 3
<strong>Output: </strong>2
<strong>Explanation: </strong>2 AND 3 = 2.</span></pre>
<p><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong>Constraints:</strong><br>1 ≤ l ≤ r ≤ 10<sup>9</sup></span></p></div><br><p><span style=font-size:18px><strong>Topic Tags : </strong><br><code>Bit Magic</code>&nbsp;<code>Data Structures</code>&nbsp;
